Learning beyond HTML

Without CSS, websites would be unoriginal

As CSS adds presentation to a website, this course will challenge and teach me beyond the fundamentals. Creating sub menus, tiered menus, designing easy and effective navigation, are styles I would like to feel comfortable with.
